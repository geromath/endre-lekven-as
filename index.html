About

Lekven Shipping As is delivering maritime consultancy services. We have extensive experience in the maritime sector and can provide Marine surveying and Supercargo services. In addition we have the knowledge to give you the best advice and tools to run your vessel the most optimal way.

We are located in Skien, Norway. But we may easily serve you worldwide, either direct or through a network of contacts.
